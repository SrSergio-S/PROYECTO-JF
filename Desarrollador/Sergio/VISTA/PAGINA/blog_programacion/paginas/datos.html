Tipe Of Data

Different types of data are handled in the pseudocode, which are:

Numerical

There are two types of numeric data:

Integers

Integers are any number that corresponds to the set of natural numbers plus their opposites, including the number zero (0).

Decimals

A decimal number is a non-integer number, made up of an integer part and a decimal part, and they are used when we want to represent numbers that are smaller than one.

Texts

There are two types of numeric data:

Chains

Character strings are also a basic data type. They consist of a sequence of characters and are used to represent text-type information, such as a person's name.

Alphanumeric

An alphanumeric character is a computer term referring to the set of numeric and alphabetic characters available to a computer. Almost exact correspondence occurs with the characters and is made up of letters, numbers, and other characters, and also they are not used for mathematical operations.
